The Battle Bus

Offered by Miller's Garage

This is our 2024 Ford Transit T-350 XLT — affectionately known as The Battle Bus. It seats up to 12 people with room in the back for luggage. It’s perfect for family road trips, group outings, airport runs, youth events, and hauling gear.

There’s a flat daily fee for personal use, or it’s free for ministry purposes. Just check the availability calendar below and reach out if it would be a help for your event or group.

Insurance note: Our policy excludes borrowed or rented use, so you’ll need to confirm that your personal auto insurance covers non-owned vehicles. We’ll send over a quick agreement to sign before you hit the road.
